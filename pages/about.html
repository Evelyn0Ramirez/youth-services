About Us

Delete the Divide created the Youth Services app to bring awareness to the mental health and community resources available in Los Angeles County. It aims to provide the youth ( 24 & younger ) of Los Angeles County with a single reference point for information and answers related to mental health, career opportunities, and local community events sourced from four LA County Departments including:


• Youth Devlopment

• Mental Health

• Child and Family Service

• Public Health

Mission Statement

The Youth Services App is a comprehensive catalog containing all resources within LA County to assist Youths. We work alongside other LA County Agencies to ensure that we can provide adequate services to the youth of LA County.
